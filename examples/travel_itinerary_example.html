Essential Travel Tips

Currency & Cards

Notify your bank before travelling. Carry small amounts of local currency (EUR/CZK). Most places accept cards, but have cash backup.

Transportation

Buy multi-day metro passes in each city (much cheaper than single tickets). Download offline maps. Book trains between cities in advance.

Museum Bookings

Pre-book popular attractions weeks in advance (Anne Frank House, Eiffel Tower summit). Save confirmation emails offline.

Dining Tips

Lunch is cheaper than dinner. Try local markets for fresh food. Restaurants near tourist sites are overpriced - walk 10 minutes away.

Safety

Watch for pickpockets in crowded areas (metro, tourist sites). Keep valuables in hotel safe. Use crossbody bags with zippers.

Communication

Download Google Translate offline. Learn basic phrases in each language. Most young people speak English in cities.
