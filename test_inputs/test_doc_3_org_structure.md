## Decision-Making Framework

### Strategic Decisions
**Decision Makers:** CEO + Executive Leadership Team
**Examples:** Market expansion, M&A, major pivots
**Approval Process:** Board review required for >$5M investments

### Product Decisions
**Decision Makers:** CPO + VP of Product + Engineering Leads
**Examples:** Feature prioritization, product roadmap
**Approval Process:** Quarterly planning cycles with executive review

### Engineering Decisions
**Decision Makers:** CTO + VP of Engineering
**Examples:** Technology stack, architecture changes
**Approval Process:** RFC process with peer review

### Resource Allocation
**Decision Makers:** CFO + Department Heads
**Examples:** Hiring plans, budget allocation
**Approval Process:** Quarterly budget reviews

## Communication Cadence

### Weekly
- Executive team meeting (Mondays, 2 hours)
- All-hands meeting (Fridays, 30 minutes)
- Department standups (daily)

### Monthly  
- Board meeting (last Thursday)
- Company all-hands with Q&A (first Friday)
- Department town halls

### Quarterly
- Strategic planning sessions (off-site, 2 days)
- OKR setting and review
- Performance reviews

### Organizational Challenges
- Maintaining culture during rapid growth
- Cross-functional collaboration at scale
- Preserving decision-making speed
- Retaining top talent in competitive market

### Mitigation Strategies
- Invest in internal communication tools
- Regular culture surveys and feedback
- Clear escalation paths for decisions
- Competitive compensation and equity packages
- Strong learning and development programs
